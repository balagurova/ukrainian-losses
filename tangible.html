Just like in the digital version, each stitch represents number of losses. The project was done in the middle of March. On that moment, one blue stitch was 15,000 refugees. One yellow stitch was one adult loss. One white stitch was one child loss.
The numbers were outdated very quickly. That is how a digital version was brought to life.
